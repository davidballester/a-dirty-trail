Knives, hammers, axes, shotguns, rifles and revolvers are weapons.
Shells and cartridges are ammunitions.

A shotgun deals between 1 and 3 damage points.
A rifle deals 1 or 2 damage points.
A revolver deals 1 damage points.
Axes deal between 0 and 2 damage points.
A knife deals 0 or 1 damage points.
A hammer deals 0 or 1 damage points.

A shotgun can carry 2 shells.
A rifle can carry 7 cartridges.
A revolver can carry 6 cartridges.

Knives, hammers and axes require close combat skill.
Shotguns, rifles and revolvers require distance combat skill.
